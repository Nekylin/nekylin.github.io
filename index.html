TEARS FOR TEARWHO!

One way or the other, we have all fallen victims to been broken by someone we love. Be it family, friends, colleagues, lovers, the pain is deepening and goes beyond the surface. So as a team, we have come together to share in your pain. We want to cry with you and be with you all through the journey of self recovery. We want to be that catalyst your heart needs to seal up. Because you mean so much to us, we will stay committed to you and not leave till you are ready to face the world again. Let us fill in that space for you!

Quote

“I’m here. I love you. I don’t care if you need to stay up crying all night long, I will stay with you. If you need to cry and scream over again, go ahead and do it—I will love you through that, as well. If you don’t need the tears, I will love you, too. There’s nothing you can ever do to lose my love. I will protect you until you are ready, and after you regain sanity, I will still protect you. I am stronger than Depression and I am braver than Loneliness and nothing will ever exhaust me.”

― Elizabeth Gilbert, Eat, Pray, Love
Sarah

Loving him was the most beautiful thing that ever happened to me. Just the mere mention of his name gave me goose pimples. I built my whole universe around him because he made me believe in forever. But my world came to an abrupt end the day he spilled the most dreaded two-lettered sentence: It's over! I felt my heart stop. For days, I was a walking dead. I became a shadow of myself. I lost hope in me and couldn't see me fitting anywhere in the society.

Ayomide

First, I couldn't believe it. I just kept thinking of the words over and over again. I couldn't sleep throughout the night. I questioned everything we shared. I wondered where I had gone wrong, what I said, how to make it better, how to improve, blamed myself and felt worthless. Then it dawned on me I had lost her and part of me died. But she never was mine!

Damian

When she told me it was over, my whole world crashed. I kept thinking of all we shared and if its ever meant anything to her. I kept having anxiety attacks. I lost confidence in myself. I felt like a coward and went straight into depression.

Stella

She was meant to be my bestfriend. She was supposed to be there for me. But on that fateful day, as I watch my supposed bestfriend marrying the man I thought loved me, I knew there was nothing left for me in this life. My whole being was thrown into vacant space. Suffered from anorexia and amnesia. I could feel my life slipping of my hands. I could do nothing to save me. But here I am. Still trying to hold on for dear life. The pain isn't going away but I am strong enough to take it all in.

Florence

He was the best thing that ever happened to me. I always told him we were stucked to each other and no matter the storm, we would fight through it together and get stronger. But eventually, I was never enough. He started giving reasons why we shouldn't even exist. And just like that, he was gone. Gone into the arms of the same woman he would always tell me not to worry about but that was just a friend. I felt lost, devastated, terrible. I didn't know who I was anymore. I was a shadow of myself. I craved for closure, hoping to come out of the never-ending dream. But none was coming my way. And it drove me even more insane.

Aisha

I'm yet to get over the fact that my mom actually left me alone to cater for my siblings after my dad's death. No pain or hurt could be greater than this. She broke me.
